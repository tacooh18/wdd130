Monkeying Around Water Rafting

Our main purpose is to share our passion for water rafting with the world in a safe and fun manner. Our secondary purpose is to build a profitable business while we at it.

History

Commercial rafting companies began to emerge in the 1960s and 1970s, which helped to develop the sport and make it more mainstream. These companies were passionate about rafting and wanted to share their love of the sport with others
